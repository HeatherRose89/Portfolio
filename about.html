Once Upon a Voice...

From the Colorado mountains to digital realms filled with stories, I’ve followed a passion for creation and connection. With a background in music business and years of experience editing podcasts, narrating audiobooks, and crafting magical content strategies, I bring a sense of wonder to every project.

As the founder of Heather Rose Virtual Services, I’ve worked with nonprofits, wellness creatives, and VR game developers—helping them reach their audience with clarity and enchantment. My philosophy? Every sound, post, and page should serve a purpose and spark something real.
